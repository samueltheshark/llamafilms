About Llama Films


Founded in 2015 in England, Llama Films is driven by my core motivation: to amplify voices often unheard. Filming and supporting individuals, particularly vulnerable youth, brings me joy and fulfills a deeper spiritual calling. This is my passion, and nothing surpasses it.

At Llama Films, we specialize in documentaries, short films, and feature films, delving into narratives of identity conflicts, human relationships, existential themes, and the exploration of faith. Our focus is on characters at society's edges, utopians, and unsung heroes.

The following film projects were conducted in Peru and England.

Documentary: "Algarrobo and the Guardians of the Forest"

Synopsis: In Peru's northern coast, the documentary 'Algarrobo and the Guardians of the Forest' reveals the struggle to protect ancient algarrobo trees from threats like pests, deforestation, and climate change. Through poignant stories and stunning imagery, it showcases the symbiotic relationship between locals and nature, urging action to preserve these vital ecosystems.

I, Too, Am Able

As director of an 8-week film course for students with learning disabilities, I assisted them in creating a mockumentary reflecting their experiences. Guiding them through all production stages, I collaborated closely with institution managers and specialist caregivers. The resulting film premiered at Cramphorn Theatre in Chelmsford, UK.

Additionally, I filmed, animated, and produced the "Reminiscence" music DVD for Broomfield Hospital's dementia ward as part of Nurture 2015.

Empowering Peruvian Youth: Workshop 2

Llama Film's second workshop, conducted over three weeks in December 2016 in Chulucanas, Peru, offered participants an immersive experience in filmmaking, covering essential skills such as storytelling, camera operation, lighting techniques, editing, and sound design.

Through hands-on exercises and mentorship, attendees gained practical insights into the art and craft of filmmaking, empowering them to express their creative visions with confidence and proficiency.
