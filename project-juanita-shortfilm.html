Juanita


Short film

A look inside the experience of Juanita, a young woman from the Peruvian countryside who is promised a better life. Sadly, her dream becomes a nightmare as she becomes a victim of human trafficking. Based on a true story.


The Short film (Draft)

It is a criminal activity that we can all witness at some point without realizing it. Human trafficking, a crime in which the victim is at the mercy of another person for exploitation such as prostitution or slavery. it is the third-largest illegal industry in the world. No country is exempt from these crimes. And yet there are clear trends. Latin America has a tradition of being among the regions with the highest number of cases.
